About Argus

Argus is a self-hosted face and object recognition service. It detects and recognizes people with InsightFace (RetinaFace + ArcFace), enrolls identities, and detects objects with YOLO and open-vocabulary Florence-2. Browse results in justified galleries, triage matches in a review queue, correct labels per face on the tagging page, hot-swap models with no restart, and drive it all over a key-authenticated REST API. Runs in Docker or natively, on CPU or GPU.
